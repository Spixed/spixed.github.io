The Tectonic Shift
in Interfaces 界面设计的
构造运动

BY ALEX BOLD // FEB 2026

We spent a decade removing the "computer" from the screen. We flattened buttons, removed borders, and floated elements in a sea of white space. But the pendulum is swinging back. 我们花了整整十年时间试图将“计算机感”从屏幕中抹去。我们拍扁了按钮，去掉了边框，让元素漂浮在白色的虚空中。但现在，钟摆正在回摆。

1. Heavy Borders

The new wave isn't about simulating reality, but about celebrating the digital container. We are seeing pure CMYK values hitting the screen. Electric Blue, Safety Orange, and Signal Green are used not just for accents, but for entire backgrounds. 新浪潮不在于模拟现实，而在于赞美数字容器本身。我们看到纯粹的 CMYK 色值冲击屏幕。电光蓝、安全橙和信号绿不仅用于点缀，更被用于整个背景。

Digital Brutalism
Is Not Ugly 数字野兽派
并不丑陋

BY SARAH INK // JAN 2026

There is a misconception that "Brutalism" means "Brutal". It comes from the French béton brut, meaning "raw concrete". It is about honesty. It is about showing the scaffolding. 人们误以为“野兽派”意味着“残暴”。其实它源自法语 béton brut，意为“清水混凝土”。它是关于诚实的艺术，是关于展示脚手架的勇气。

2. Raw Structure

When we use default fonts and visible grids, we strip away the marketing veneer. We ask the user to look at the content, naked and unadorned. 当我们使用默认字体和可见网格时，我们剥离了营销的伪装。我们在邀请用户注视内容本身——赤裸且未经修饰。
